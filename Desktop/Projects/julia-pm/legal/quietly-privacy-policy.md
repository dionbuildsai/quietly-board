## About Us

Quietly Systems Inc. ("Quietly", "we", "us") is a technology company incorporated in Quebec, Canada. We provide a property management automation platform to property management companies ("Clients").

## What This Policy Covers

This policy describes how Quietly collects and handles personal information in two contexts:

1. **Client information** — information about our business clients (property managers)
2. **Tenant information** — personal information of our clients' tenants, which we process on behalf of our clients

## Client Information

We collect and use the following information about our clients:

- **Business contact details:** name, email, phone, company name
- **Billing information:** invoicing details (we do not store credit card numbers)
- **Account credentials:** authentication credentials for platform access

We use this information to provide our services, issue invoices, and communicate about service matters. We retain client information for the duration of the business relationship and for a reasonable period afterward for legal and accounting purposes.

## Tenant Information

We process tenant personal information strictly on behalf of our clients and under written data processing agreements. Our clients are the data controllers; we are the service provider (processor).

- We process tenant information solely to provide our platform services
- We do not sell, rent, or share tenant information for marketing or any other purpose
- We do not use tenant information to train AI models
- We engage subprocessors to assist in providing our services (see our client agreements for the current subprocessor list)
- We implement security measures including encrypted data transmission, restricted access controls, and secure hosting

For information about how a specific property manager handles your personal information as a tenant, please contact your property manager directly.

## Security

We maintain reasonable security measures to protect personal information, including:

- Encrypted data transmission (TLS)
- Restricted server access via SSH key authentication
- Database systems not exposed to the public internet
- Regular data backups

## Your Rights

Under Quebec's Act Respecting the Protection of Personal Information in the Private Sector, you have the right to access, correct, and request deletion of your personal information, and to file a complaint with the Commission d'acces a l'information du Quebec (CAI).

**Tenants:** Please contact your property manager to exercise your rights. Your property manager is the data controller.

**Clients:** Contact us directly using the information below.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be communicated to clients via email.
