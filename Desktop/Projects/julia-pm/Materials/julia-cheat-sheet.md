## What Happens When a Tenant Reaches Out

1. Tenant sends a message (Telegram, WhatsApp, SMS, Email, or Phone)
2. The AI bot responds automatically — asks clarifying questions (up to 3 exchanges)
3. A **ticket** is created on your dashboard
4. You receive a **Telegram notification** with the ticket summary
5. If urgent — notification includes **dispatch buttons** to contact a vendor

## Common Actions

### View a ticket conversation
Tickets → click a ticket → see all messages + photos on the right

### Pause the bot and reply manually
1. Open a ticket → click **Bot OFF** (top right)
2. Type your message in the input box → Send
3. When done, leave the page — bot resumes automatically

### Close a ticket
Open a ticket → click **Mark Resolved** (top right)

### Delete a message
Hover over any message → click the red **X** → confirm

### Delete a ticket
Open a ticket → click **Delete** (top right) → confirm
*(This removes all messages, photos, and files associated with it)*

## Telegram Notifications

You receive notifications on Telegram for:
- Every new ticket created
- Urgent tickets (with dispatch buttons)

To change which Telegram account receives notifications:
Settings → Telegram → change **Owner Chat ID** → Save → enter the 6-digit code sent to Telegram

---

## What the Bot Does NOT Do

- It does NOT call emergency services
- It does NOT promise timelines or send contractors without your approval
- It does NOT respond to tenants whose phone/email isn't in the system
- If the AI can't handle something, it logs the ticket and you take over manually
